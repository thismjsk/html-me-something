Poems About Pizza

This is exactly what is sounds like.

A wise man once said, "Poetry is a lot like pizza. It's a canvas in which one works true art."

On a foggy November afternoon, a close friend of mine thought aloud, "I have yet to be awestruck by the celebration of pizza conveyed through poetry. I nodded, and thought...could I embrace this challenge and convey pizza's majesty?

I shall do my best.

By: Jonathan McHambles
